Job Title: Junior Data Scientist
Location: Bangalore, India
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role
We are looking for a motivated Junior Data Scientist to join our analytics team and support the design, development, and deployment of data-driven solutions. You will work closely with senior data scientists, data engineers, and business stakeholders to turn raw data into actionable insights.

Responsibilities
- Clean, preprocess, and analyze structured and unstructured data using Python, SQL, and relevant libraries.
- Build and evaluate predictive models (classification and regression) using machine learning techniques.
- Develop analytical dashboards and visualizations to communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders.
- Collaborate with product and business teams to define metrics, design experiments, and measure impact.
- Document data pipelines, experiments, and model performance in a clear and reproducible way.
- Contribute to improving existing models by monitoring performance and suggesting enhancements.

Required Skills and Qualifications
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, or a related field.
- Strong programming skills in Python and familiarity with libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn.
- Solid understanding of machine learning fundamentals (classification, regression, model evaluation).
- Good knowledge of SQL for querying relational databases.
- Experience with data visualization tools or libraries (e.g., Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI, Tableau).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail.
- Good verbal and written communication skills; ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical audiences.

Preferred Skills
- Exposure to NLP techniques (text preprocessing, TF-IDF, embeddings).
- Experience working with large datasets and basic knowledge of big data ecosystems (e.g., Spark) is a plus.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and model deployment concepts.
- Knowledge of A/B testing and experimental design.

Keywords
Python, SQL, Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, Machine Learning, Classification, Regression, Data Visualization, Power BI, Tableau, Statistics, NLP, Predictive Modeling, Model Evaluation, Dashboards, Junior Data Scientist, Bangalore
